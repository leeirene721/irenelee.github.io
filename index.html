About Me

Irene Lee is a student majoring in Design and Environmental Analysis in the College of Human Ecology at Cornell University. As a problem solver and aspiring designer, Irene wants to respond to human needs by improving the built environment. Irene is interested in product design and ergonomics and believes that research and design thinking can make small changes that grow into impactful ideas.
